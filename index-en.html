About the project

As a part of the global Photo Vogue initiative and to support and promote the creative talents of Ukraine in the international arena and to discover new artistic voices, Vogue Ukraine is launching a new initiative in contemporary art photography and creating a unique digital platform at vogue.ua.

Photo Vogue Ukraine Futurespective Open Call is a contest of young and emerging talents specialising in fashion and art photography. It is open to those whose work has already appeared on Vogue Ukraine's pages and to new names waiting to be discovered.

The Vogue Ukraine team will select 12 best portfolios. This shortlist will then be presented to the members of the jury, which includes international and Ukrainian artists, curators and photographers.

As part of this year's Futurespective initiative, Vogue Ukraine will host a photo exhibition in February 2025 in London. The show will include the works of this year’s winners and selected works of contestants. The winner of the main prize will be granted a special project to be published in the print edition of the magazine in 2025.

How to participate

Submissions are accepted as a link to a folder on Google Drive.

Technical requirements — format: .jpg, size: up to 50 MB, number of photos: 5-10.

A text file with information about the author and each submitted work should be added to the folder in the following format: photo preview, photographer's name, work title, date and place where it was taken.

Send a link to the folder to Futurespective@vogue.ua.

The cover letter should include the artist’s real and creative name, age, place of birth, citizenship and current residence, and a link to Instagram account and/or a professional website. In addition, please provide brief information about yourself: how long you have been practicing in photography and your plans and expectations for participating in the contest.

Terms of the competition

December 2024: general submission is open through January 1 2025.

January 2025: announcement of the shortlist of finalists

January 2025: determination of the winner of the competition

February 2025: Vogue Ukraine Futurespective 2024 exhibition in London
